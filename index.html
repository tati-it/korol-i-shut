History

The band was formed in 1988 by school friends in Saint Petersburg around founder members Mikhail Gorshok Gorshenev, Alexandr Balu Balunov and Alexandr Lieutenant Shchigoliev. Singer Andrei Kniaz Kniazev joined the band in 1990 and guitarist Yakov Tsvirkunov in 1997. The band's name, which means The King and the Jester, was adopted in 1992
